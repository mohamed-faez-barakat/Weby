Weby

Who are we?

Weby is an educational platform designed to help beginners dive into the world of front-end development. Our goal is to make HTML, CSS, and JavaScript accessible, understandable, and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you’re just starting or brushing up on fundamentals, Weby offers clear explanations, hands-on examples, and step-by-step tutorials to build your coding confidence.

Our Mission

Weby aims to create an engaging, self-paced learning environment for front-end developers. Our tutorials focus on real-world applications and essential coding skills, allowing users to learn and experiment with code as they go. We’re committed to providing foundational skills that enable our users to build modern, responsive, and visually appealing websites.

What We Use

Weby focuses on core front-end technologies: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Each lesson and example has been crafted with these languages to ensure that our users develop a strong foundation. These languages form the backbone of front-end development, and mastering them opens the door to more advanced frameworks and techniques.

What’s Next?

Our vision is to expand Weby into a comprehensive front-end learning platform. In the future, we plan to add more interactive exercises, advanced lessons, and possibly explore popular frameworks like React and Vue. We’re dedicated to growing and adapting to meet the needs of our users as they advance in their coding journey.
